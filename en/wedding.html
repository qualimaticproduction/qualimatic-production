✻ FAQ for international couples

I work with your wedding planner as the bilingual intermediary, ensuring smooth, high-quality production. This is the most efficient setup for destination weddings — your planner handles communication in English, and I focus entirely on capturing the day.
Yes. Across the Loire Valley there's no travel fee. Elsewhere in France — Provence, Bordeaux, Burgundy, the French Alps — and abroad, travel and accommodation are quoted upfront, at real cost, with no hidden margin. You can see every region and château I cover here.
Yes. When you work with a planner, they usually handle communication in English while I focus entirely on filming — the smoothest setup for a destination wedding. Without a planner, we keep things simple, directly between us, in clear English.
Within the Loire Valley, travel is included. Anywhere further — across France or abroad — travel and accommodation are quoted transparently, at cost. As an EU-based supplier, there are no visa issues for weddings in Europe.
I recommend booking 12–18 months in advance for château weddings in Loire Valley, as iconic venues fill up quickly. For 2027 and 2028, the diary is already opening — early conversations are warmly welcomed.
