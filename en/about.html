MY NAME IS Charly

✻MY APPROACH

Films that imitate nothing.

I believe in films that imitate nothing.

In true emotions, captured without noise.

In the light that says what words leave unsaid.

My craft: to disappear, the better to tell.

Be yourselves — I'll take care of making it a film.

✻MY PRINCIPLES

Four pillars, one standard.

They guide every film, whatever the world.

01

Absolute discretion

Present without imposing. You won't see me — you'll see yourselves, later, sincere.

02

Natural light

No flashes, no artifice. I work with what the day offers — and that's enough.

03

True emotion

I don't direct. I capture what is, in the moment, without artificial guidance.

04

Timeless cinema

Your films don't follow trends. They cross time, like your memories.

✻WHAT I'M NOT

To know if I'm made to work with you.

  • A videographer juggling multiple weddings the same weekend.
  • A vendor chasing TikTok trends.
  • An operator who waits to be told what to shoot.
  • A technician who delivers raw footage.
  • Someone who draws attention on the day.

I AM

  • A director who chooses projects with care.
  • An author who defends a cinematic vision.
  • An observer who senses moments before they happen.
  • A craftsman who delivers finished, lasting films.
  • A discreet witness who steps aside for emotion.
